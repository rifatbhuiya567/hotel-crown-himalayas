about us

Hotel Crown Himalayas, Pokhara - Nepal: Your Himalayan Getaway Awaits

Crown Himalayas offers scrumptious Indian, Continental and Chinese Cuisine. Our experienced chefs cook mouthwatering dishes and ensure you Nepali experience.

Hotel Crown Himalayas boasts some stunning views of the Annapurna Himalayas, Phewa Lake as well as easy access to the thriving lake street of Pokhara. We are situated adjacent to the Phewa Lake, a mere 3 KMS away from Pokhara domestic airport. We offer 40 rooms(All deluxe rooms, categorised with single, double and suite), restaurants, meeting rooms decorated with comfort and elegance in mind.

We are very near from the airport of Pokhara and has very easy facility of transportation.
